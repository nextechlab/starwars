A NEW HOPE FOR ENGINEERS

It is a period of recession.

The market has been hit badly by the dark side, leading to shortage of jobs for engineers.

Java and C++ are now endangered languages and Python has taken over.

Meanwhile, Master Yoda trains a bunch of students from SRM University to develop Artificially Intelligent systems to defeat the Dark Side.

The students have mastered the art of Machine Learning and Neural Networks and defeated many stormtroopers in hackathons.

Also possessing the knowledge of Mixed Reality and the sorcery of Circuits, they are willing to take over the Dark Side when the time comes.
